Best of MAC Lips Set 2024: The Ultimate Lip Collection You Need This Year

As a true beauty lover, we all know that no one does lip products quite like MAC Cosmetics. Known for their iconic shades, stunning finishes, and lasting formulas, MAC lipsticks have been a staple in makeup bags for decades. This year, MAC has released their highly anticipated Best of MAC Lips Set 2024, and let me tell you—it’s a game-changer.

What’s Inside the Best of MAC Lips Set 2024?

This year’s lip set is curated with both cult classics and new releases that cater to every mood and occasion. Whether you’re a fan of bold colors or neutral nudes, this collection has something for everyone.

  • Ruby Woo (Retro Matte)
    • Finish: Retro Matte
    • Shade: Vivid Blue-Red
    • Price (Single): $24

    Ruby Woo is the ultimate red lipstick, flattering on every skin tone. The retro matte finish is ultra-matte but still comfortable on the lips, with a long-lasting formula that stays put all day.

    Styling Tip: Pair Ruby Woo with a simple cat-eye liner and a touch of highlighter for a classic, timeless look.

  • Velvet Teddy (Matte)
    • Finish: Matte
    • Shade: Deep-Tone Beige
    • Price (Single): $24

    Velvet Teddy is the ultimate nude for everyday wear. The matte finish gives it a chic, sophisticated edge without being overly drying.

    Styling Tip: Layer Velvet Teddy over MAC’s Whirl Lip Liner for a defined, fuller lip look.
