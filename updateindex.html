Best of MAC Lips Set 2024: The Ultimate Lip Collection You Need This Year

As a true beauty lover, we all know that no one does lip products quite like MAC Cosmetics. Known for their iconic shades, stunning finishes, and lasting formulas, MAC lipsticks have been a staple in makeup bags for decades. This year, MAC has released their highly anticipated Best of MAC Lips Set 2024, and let me tell you—it’s a game-changer.

In this post, I’ll take you through the must-have shades and finishes included in this year’s set, how to style them, and why they’re absolute essentials for your makeup collection.

What’s Inside the Best of MAC Lips Set 2024?

This year’s lip set is curated with both cult classics and new releases that cater to every mood and occasion. Whether you’re a fan of bold colors or neutral nudes, this collection has something for everyone.

  • Ruby Woo (Retro Matte)
    • Finish: Retro Matte
    • Shade: Vivid Blue-Red
    • Price (Single): $24

      • You can’t talk about MAC without mentioning their legendary Ruby Woo. Known as the ultimate red lipstick, this shade is flattering on every skin tone and is perfect for those days when you want to feel powerful and confident. The retro matte finish is ultra-matte but still comfortable on the lips, with a long-lasting formula that stays put all day.

      Styling Tip: Pair Ruby Woo with a simple cat-eye liner and a touch of highlighter for a classic, timeless look.
    • Velvet Teddy (Matte)
      • Finish: Matte
      • Shade: Deep-Tone Beige
      • Price (Single): $24
        • For a stunning nude that works for everyday wear, Velvet Teddy is the ultimate go-to. It’s the perfect balance of warm and neutral tones, making it ideal for both day and night looks. The matte finish gives it a chic, sophisticated edge without being overly drying. Styling Tip: Layer Velvet Teddy over MAC’s Whirl Lip Liner for a defined, fuller lip look.
